Cancer: A Wholeness Revolution

The Core Manifesto

This is not a linear illness narrative. It explores wholeness, healing, and identity through the experience of illness—challenging the performative, combative paradigms of recovery.

The book untangles the vital distinction between collapse and surrender. Collapse drains energy and produces rumination. Surrender allows presence, open-heartedness, and a return to the body's native intelligence.

Moving through the landscapes of emotional anatomy, internal parts, boundaries, and lineage, Cancer: A Wholeness Revolution is a direct map of self-leadership. It is a call to step out of inherited dynamics and return home to your own system.
